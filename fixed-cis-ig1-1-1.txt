Purpose: Establish and maintain an accurate inventory of all enterprise assets with the potential to store or process data, including end-user devices, network devices, IoT devices, and servers. This provides visibility into your environment and serves as the foundation for security controls.

Implementation: - Implement automated asset discovery tools to continuously scan the network
- Include all enterprise assets: end-user devices, network devices, IoT devices, servers
- Record key information: network address, hardware address, machine name, owner, department
- Mark assets as approved or unapproved for network connection
- For mobile devices, use MDM solutions to track and manage devices
- Include both physical and virtual assets, including cloud resources
- Review and update the inventory at least bi-annually
